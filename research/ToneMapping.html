Tone Mapping

Tone Mapping，也就是色调映射，将HDR图像的高动态范围映射到指定的范围内，方便在普通显示器上展示和用人眼查看。

相关代码

1、 "pfstmo" tone mapping library，该库是ToneMapping算法的C++实现，可贵的是提供了源代码：http://pfstools.sourceforge.net/pfstmo.html，目前最新的版本是1.9，主要实现的算法列表如下所示：

  • Adaptive logarithmic tone mapping operator, based on Drago, F., Myszkowski, K., Annen, T., Chiba, N. (2003). Adaptive Logarithmic Mapping For Displaying High Contrast Scenes. Computer Graphics Forum, 22(3), 419–426.
  • Fast bilateral filtering tone mapping operator, based on Durand, F., and Dorsey, J. (2002). Fast bilateral filtering for the display of high-dynamic-range images. ACM Transactions on Graphics, 21(3), 257–266.
  • Gradient domain tone mapping operator, based on Fattal, R., Lischinski, D., & Werman, M. (2002). Gradient domain high dynamic range compression. ACM Transactions on Graphics, 21(3), 249.
  • Contrast domain tone mapping operator, based on Mantiuk, R., Myszkowski, K., & Seidel, H. (2006). A perceptual framework for contrast processing of high dynamic range images. ACM Transactions on Applied Perception (TAP), 3(3), 286–308.
  • Display adaptive tone mapping, based on Mantiuk, R., Daly, S., & Kerofsky, L. (2008). Display adaptive tone mapping. ACM Transactions on Graphics (Proc. of SIGGRAPH), 27(3), 68.
  • Time-dependent visual adaptation tone mapping operator, based on Pattanaik, S. N., Tumblin, J., Yee, H., & Greenberg, D. P. (2000). Time-dependent visual adaptation for fast realistic image display. ACM Transactions on Graphics, 47–54.
  • Photographic tone mapping operator, based on Reinhard, E., Stark, M., Shirley, P., & Ferwerda, J. (2002). Photographic tone reproduction for digital images. ACM Transactions on Graphics.
  • Photoreceptor tone mapping operator, based on Reinhard, E., & Devlin, K. (2005). Dynamic range reduction inspired by photoreceptor physiology. IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics, 11(1), 13–24.
